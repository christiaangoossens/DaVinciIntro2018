Do you want to be the next Robin Hood?

ESH Da Vinci is the student archery club of the TU/e and Fontys in Eindhoven.
We have members of all levels, and for beginners we offer an introductory course into archery.

Come join us for our introductory course,
or for one of our regular activities and social drinks.
